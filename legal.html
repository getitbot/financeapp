Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 01, 2026

At Finance Buddha, we value your trust and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy provides you with details about the manner in which your data is collected, stored, and used by us. You are advised to read this Privacy Policy carefully.

1. Information Collection

We collect personal information such as your name, mailing address, phone number, and email address when you apply for a loan or sign up for our services. We may also collect financial information like PAN card number and bank account details for processing loan applications.

2. Use of Information

  • To process your loan application with our partner banks.
  • To send you updates regarding your application status.
  • To improve our services and customize your experience.

3. Data Security

We use strict security measures to protect your data. All sensitive financial data is encrypted using SSL technology. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

RBI Guidelines & Fair Practice

Finance Buddha complies with the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) guidelines on Digital Lending. We ensure transparency in all our dealings.

Key Compliance Measures:

  • Transparency: All fees, charges, and interest rates are clearly communicated to the borrower before loan processing via a Key Fact Statement (KFS).
